Women safety in India is widely discussed everywhere nowadays. It has now become a major issue. The crime rate is on the spike. Women are neither safe outside nor at home. so team "Yati:who strives with pertinacity of purpose"have come with a women safety website named "Safetycop"which intends to provide safety to women by providing features like self-defence instruction for women to acquaint girls with basic self-defence moves that will help them when in trouble,email popups will be send to their family during trouble,gps location can be grabbed and shared,troubleshoot tips and emergency services will be provided as per requirement.our main motive is to provide a fearless life which contains no trepidation to the endangered women becoz "women strong,country strong" as they are the social conscience of a country.
